# Noetica Beta — Feedback Form Template

Built to mirror Google Forms field types, so you can copy this straight in.
Goal: keep **bugs**, **feature requests**, and **general usability feedback** in separate buckets from the start — easier to act on later than sorting one big comment thread.

---

### Form title
**Noetica Beta — Personalized Email Feedback**

### Form description
Thanks for trying the personalized email demo. This takes about 3 minutes. Honest answers — including "this didn't work" or "I didn't get it" — are exactly what's useful right now.

---

## Section 1 — Your experience

**1. Did you receive the personalized email?**
*(Multiple choice)*
- Yes
- No
- Not sure

**2. How relevant did the email feel to the preferences you selected?**
*(Linear scale, 1–5: 1 = Not relevant at all, 5 = Very relevant)*

**3. Was anything in the email unclear or hard to understand?**
*(Paragraph, optional)*

---

## Section 2 — Bugs / things that didn't work
*(Technical problems only — not missing features. Those go in Section 3.)*

**4. Did you run into any errors or technical issues?**
*(Multiple choice)*
- Yes
- No

**5. If yes — what happened? Please describe what you expected vs. what actually happened.**
*(Paragraph, optional — show only if Q4 = Yes, if your form supports conditional logic)*

**6. Screenshot of the issue, if you have one**
*(File upload, optional)*

---

## Section 3 — Feature requests / what's missing
*(Things that worked fine, but you wish existed or worked differently.)*

**7. Is there anything you wish the email included that it didn't?**
*(Paragraph, optional)*

**8. Any other ways you'd want to set your preferences, beyond the form you already filled out?**
*(Paragraph, optional)*

---

## Section 4 — Anything unexpected
**9. Did you use this, or think about using it, in a way we probably didn't anticipate?**
*(Paragraph, optional)*

**10. Anything else on your mind — good or critical?**
*(Paragraph, optional)*

---

## Section 5 — Wrap-up

**11. Overall, how useful was this for you?**
*(Linear scale, 1–5: 1 = Not useful, 5 = Very useful)*

**12. Okay to follow up if we have a question about your answer?**
*(Multiple choice)*
- Yes — here's my email/contact: *(Short answer, conditional)*
- No, that's fine

---

## Notes
- Sections 2 and 3 are intentionally separate questions, not one big "tell us what you think" box — bug reports need fixing now, feature requests get triaged later. Mixing them slows both down.
- Most fields are marked optional on purpose — a tester who only answers 3 questions has still given you something usable; forcing every field tends to lower completion rates.
- If your Google Form supports section branching, routing Q5–Q6 to only show when Q4 = "Yes" keeps the form feeling shorter than it looks.
