## LinkedIn Post

I've been building Noetica — an open-source attempt at a different kind of research feed.

Most systems rank by engagement: what's trending, what's popular, what gets clicks. Noetica ranks by scientific significance instead — novelty, strength of evidence, cross-domain influence, historical importance. Every morning it scans new papers, patents, grants, and repositories, and builds a personalized briefing around three things you tell it: how much time you actually have, your level of expertise, and which fields you care about.

One detail worth mentioning: 20% of what you receive is deliberately outside your stated interests — a small allocation so your feed doesn't turn into an echo chamber.
