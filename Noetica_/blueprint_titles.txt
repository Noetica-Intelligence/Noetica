=== Noetica Master Blueprint Part 12.pdf ===
NOETICA MASTER BLUEPRINT
PART 12
Scientific Consensus, Debate & Truth Assessment Engine
Purpose
One of the biggest problems in science is that:
Publication ≠ Truth
Citation ≠ Truth
Popularity ≠ Truth
Media Coverage ≠ Truth
Scientific progress often occurs through disagreement, replication, criticism, failed experiments,
alternative theories, and gradual convergence toward better explanations.
Noetica must therefore avoid a major failure mode:
Treating all published knowledge as equally reliable.
The purpose of this engine is to continuously assess:
How much evidence exists?
