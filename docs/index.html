Welcome to Noetica, the canonical reference manual documenting the architectural orchestration, ingestion pipelines, and structural philosophy powering the Noetica Intelligence Engine.

The Mission

Build an open-source scientific intelligence platform that continuously discovers, ranks, connects, explains, and forecasts the most important developments in human knowledge across all disciplines — using evidence-based signals, not popularity signals.

Why Most Research Digest Systems Fail

500 papers/day → unread
 50 papers/day → still unread
 10 papers/day → mostly irrelevant

The problem is not volume. The problem is the wrong output.

Wrong model: Input: User interests → Output: Research papers
Noetica model: Input: User interests + Expertise + Reading time + Career goals → Output: Ranked discoveries + Summaries + Why it matters + Future impact + Cross-field connections
