## Notes

- **Privacy line worth adding to the form itself:** since this collects email + preferences, a one-line note on the form ("used only to send your test digest, nothing else") fits Noetica's privacy-by-design principle and tends to increase sign-up trust.
- **On scarcity:** avoid lines like "only 10 spots left" — if you do want to cap testers, say the real number honestly (e.g. "I can only onboard a small batch first") rather than implying artificial urgency.
- **Collecting feedback:** consider linking a second short form (or just asking for WhatsApp/DM replies) so feedback comes back structured — separates bugs from feature requests early, per the beta program approach.
