02 / The Three Timelines TEMPORAL SCOPE

Noetica categorizes discoveries into three distinct temporal tiers running in parallel. This ensures that the engine distinguishes between immediate breakthroughs and historical foundations.

Timeline Scope Core Question
Foundational5,000+ yearsWhat changed civilization?
ModernLast 50 yearsWhat changed science?
EmergingLast 5 yearsWhat might change the future?

Example: Foundational Tier 0 Breakthroughs

Discoveries in this tier possess a 10/10 Civilization Score. For instance:

  • Zero: No algebra, calculus, computers, AI without it.
  • Calculus: Enables modern engineering, physics, and ML algorithms.
  • Electromagnetism: Without Maxwell, there is no radio, internet, or computers.
  • Relativity: GPS systems fail without Einstein's equations.
  • Quantum Mechanics: Foundation for semiconductors, lasers, and MRI machines.

The Discovery Entity

The unit of analysis in Noetica is not a paper. It is a Discovery. 

Discovery (e.g., CRISPR)
│
├── Papers (thousands)
├── Patents
├── Grants
├── Datasets
├── Conferences
├── Startups
├── Products
└── Researchers

A paper is merely a leaf on the tree. The Discovery is the tree itself, persisting across the three timelines.
